Welcome to Brindavan

The sacred land of Krishna's childhood and divine love

About the City

Brindavan, located on the banks of the Yamuna in Uttar Pradesh, India, is where Lord Krishna spent his early life. The city is alive with bhajans, temples, peacocks, and a sense of divine bliss. It’s not just a city — it’s a sacred experience of devotion and eternal love.

Top Attractions in Brindavan

Banke Bihari Temple

One of the holiest temples, where devotees lose themselves in the darshan of the playful Krishna.

Kesi Ghat

Yamuna river ghat where Krishna killed the demon Kesi — evening aarti here is unforgettable.

Holi in Brindavan

Experience the world-famous flower Holi and color celebrations of Radha and Krishna's eternal love.

Culture & Festivals

Brindavan is soaked in bhakti. Every street sings Krishna's name, every corner echoes with kirtan. Major festivals like Janmashtami, Radhashtami, and Holi are celebrated with flowers, chants, and joy. Devotees come from around the world to live the divine culture of this sacred city.
